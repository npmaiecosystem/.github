## 🎯 &nbsp; What Is NPMAI ECOSYSTEM?

</div>

> **NPMAI ECOSYSTEM** is an open-source, multi-domain AI research and development organization founded in Kota, Rajasthan, India. We build free AI tools for developers worldwide, conduct original research across five domains, and believe that cutting-edge technology should have zero cost barriers.

> We operate at the intersection of **Artificial Intelligence**, **Mathematics**, **Chemistry**, **Political Science**, and **Constitutional Law** — because we believe the future needs researchers who don't stay in lanes.
